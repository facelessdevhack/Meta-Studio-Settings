How do you define your style?

Neo Ancient Tribal Future, the time merger
between the transhuman archetype vizion
of the future, entangled within the dissolution
of the human soul isolated from the source
of the ethereal consciousness

Who are you?

A mere specter, a collection of atoms in transient to a
higher source, meanwhile encapsulated in a mammalian
brain giving meaning to things that matter and also
that doesn't matter. Perhaps photography helps me to
see this clearly by helping me step out of this low
dimensional reality

What are you inspirations?

My inspiration comes from the frustration to
understand the mystery of life, reenacting
emotions, persuing the intangible cryptic
language of life.

The merger between the ancient and future,
gives birth to the Future Primitive.
